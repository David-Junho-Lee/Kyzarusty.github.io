Bootcamp Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I want to use the skills I gain during this course to become a programmer who can work in any environment given. I would love to be a part of a team with an innovative mind and work towards improving people's quality of life. Programming is fun and challenging; I would like it to stay that way so I can live a dream life where I have a job that I enjoy!

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in bootcamp?

My strengths are creativity, attention to detail, and innovation. They are helpful in learning, but I believe my tenacity for problem-solving is my biggest strength, when I'm stuck on a problem, I don't stop until I figure out. During the foundation, I discovered I lack skills in time management. As the BootCamp is structured to have a specific time for lecture and pair programming, my lack of time management skills will not be a significant limitation as it did during the foundation. But it is something I really need to work on during the Bootcamp. My other limitation would be I struggle when there are no challenges to motivate me, but programming is all about challenges, so that It won't hinder me during the Bootcamp.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I would have a lot of non-technical challenges during the Bootcamp, as I'm an introvert who is not a social person. I would be doing pair programming daily with different people, and speaking in front of the group might make me nervous. Although I tend to work well in a team environment, it will still be a challenge.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I want to develop my communication skills and improve my self-awareness and reflective skills. I would say I'm pretty weak in Emotional intelligence, which is why I chose Dev-Academy over other choices. I believe it is more important than my coding skills as it is essential in a team environment, especially in a real-life job.

What are your expectations from the Boocamp Team?

I expect the Bootcamp team to be mentors. Provide guidance, motivation, constructive feedback, and role modeling to help keep me on track.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

I know that the Bootcamp will be very intensive and can be overwhelming at times. I will seek help from others and facilitators before I'm overloaded and communicate consistently about how I feel and am doing. 

I would eat well, work out, and have a good rest to maintain a healthy body and mind, work hard, and I will make my efforts to communicate with others and facilitators to work productively and safely.

I want myself to be entirely focused on the Bootcamp and learn everything I can from others and facilitators so that I won't have any regret after the course is finished.
